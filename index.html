Summary

Flux uses Supabase for account, squad, feed, challenge, mission, reward, and beta sync data. Apple Health access is optional and only happens after user permission. Raw Apple Health samples are not intended to be uploaded during the beta.

Visibility

Pulse posts can be Public, Friends, or Squad depending on the visibility selected by the user. Squad activity may be visible to members of the same squad.

Health And Medical Notice

Flux is for wellness accountability and habit tracking. Flux is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
