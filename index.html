Privacy Policy

Flux is a social wellness accountability app for adults aged 18 and above. This page explains what data Flux collects, how it is used, and how to contact us.

Read the full policy Community Guidelines Terms Support Contact

Summary

Flux uses Supabase for account, squad, feed, challenge, reward, terms-acceptance, report, and moderation data. Apple Health access is optional and only happens after user permission. Raw Apple Health samples and optional Flux Ring history remain on the device.

Visibility

Pulse posts can be Public, Friends, or Squad depending on the visibility selected by the user. Squad activity may be visible to members of the same squad.

Safety And Control

Every post has an authenticated author. Flux provides reporting, immediate post hiding, bilateral blocking, filtering, and human moderation. Users can permanently delete their account in the app.

Health And Medical Notice

Flux is for wellness accountability and habit tracking. Flux is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
