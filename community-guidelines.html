Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 11, 2026

OverviewPrivacySupport

Be supportive

Encourage progress without harassment, bullying, humiliation, threats, or unwanted pressure.

Keep it appropriate

Do not post hateful, sexually explicit, violent, dangerous, abusive, or illegal content. Do not encourage self-harm or harm to others.

Protect privacy

Share only information you have the right to share. Never expose another person’s private health, identity, or contact information.

No spam or deception

Do not impersonate people, manipulate engagement, distribute scams, or post repetitive promotions.

Reports and blocks

Use the post menu to report content or users. Reports are private. Blocking removes social visibility and interaction between the two accounts. Manage blocked users under Profile > Settings > Privacy & Safety.

Flux uses limited automated filtering as an initial safeguard. It is not perfect and does not replace reports or human review. Contact realfluxapp@gmail.com for safety concerns.
