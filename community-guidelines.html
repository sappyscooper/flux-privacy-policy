Zero Tolerance

Flux does not permit objectionable, threatening, hateful, sexually explicit, illegal, harassing, abusive, exploitative, deceptive, or privacy-invasive content or abusive users.

Be Supportive

Encourage progress without shaming, bullying, threats, unwanted pressure, or targeted harassment. Do not promote dangerous wellness practices or present wellness estimates as medical advice.

Protect Privacy

Share only information and images you have the right to share. Never expose another person’s private, identifying, health, or location information. Challenge proof is visible only to authorized Squad participants and moderators.

No Impersonation, Spam, Or Scams

Do not impersonate people, manipulate engagement, distribute scams, or repeatedly post promotions or irrelevant content.

Reports, Hiding, And Blocks

Use the safety menu to privately report posts, comments, profiles, Squads, challenges, or challenge proof. Reports receive a server-generated 24-hour response deadline. You can hide a post immediately and block an account in both directions.

Enforcement

Flux may hide or remove content, warn or temporarily suspend an account, or permanently eject an offending account. Filtering is an initial safeguard and does not replace reports or human review.
