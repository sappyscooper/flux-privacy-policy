Contact

Email realfluxapp@gmail.com for support, privacy requests, account deletion assistance, appeals, or urgent safety concerns.

In-App Safety Tools

On another user’s Pulse, open the three-dot menu to Report Post, Hide Post, Report User, or Block User. Comments, profiles, challenges, and challenge proof also provide report actions. Manage blocked users under Profile > Settings > Safety & Support.

Delete Your Account

Open Profile > Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently deletes the authentication identity and associated account data, subject to limited backup, security, legal, and moderation-record retention.

Report Response

Reports are private and receive a server-generated 24-hour response deadline. For imminent danger, contact the appropriate local emergency service; Flux is not an emergency or medical service.
