Support

Help with Flux accounts, safety, privacy, and app features.

OverviewPrivacyGuidelines

Contact

Email realfluxapp@gmail.com. Include the Flux username and a short description of the issue, but never send passwords or Apple Health details.

Account deletion

Delete an account in Flux under Profile > Settings > Privacy & Safety > Delete Account. Contact support if the in-app flow cannot be completed.

Safety

Use the menu on another user’s Pulse to report a post, report an account, or block the user. Reports are private.

Health notice

Apple Health is optional. Flux is not a medical app and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
